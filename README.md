## About

I am an MSc Data Science candidate at the National University of Singapore, specializing in the sustainability track. My background combines economics training, finance coursework, securities research, strategy analysis, quantitative modeling, and AI-enabled research systems.

I use data science to make finance-facing research more structured and testable: factor construction, return prediction, regression modeling, risk-aware validation, ESG and climate-transition analysis, and retrieval-augmented document workflows.

This profile is a curated portfolio of academic projects and research prototypes. Finance-related repositories use simulated backtests or paper-trading environments only and are not investment advice, live trading records, or fundraising materials.
